Features & Benefits

From tracking your bus real-time on the web to getting alerts as soon as the bus deviates from the defined location, School Bus Tracking makes transportation significantly safer.

Tracking

It is the primary feature required in any bus tracking system is knowing the real-time location of the vehicle. The exact location can be known by integrating GPS technology into the system.

Emergency

The driver can sent a message to a parents if any emergency occur in a bus.Any change in pickup or drop timings is sent via notification through SMS.

Admin

The tracking software allows the management to organize the routes according to their convenience. They can designate buses on various routes by keeping the number of students in mind.

Parents

The parents can have access to both the real-time and historical travelling details of the buses. It helps them analyze the useful routing data helping them to spot any unusual activity

Driver

A bus tracking system allows drivers to boost their efficiency in different ways. With route scheduling and optimization, they can reach the destination in less time.

Security

The system will proactively send notifications and alerts to the parents’ registered mobile number. Any change in pickup or drop timings or pre-determined route is sent via notification through SMS or app notification.
